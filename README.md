## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🌿 Xarhani — Local & Rural Producer Marketplace
> *Mobile application · Flutter · Firebase · Google Maps API*

A mobile platform designed to give visibility and market access to local and rural producers. Xarhani bridges the gap between small-scale producers and consumers through an intuitive, accessibility-first interface. It features an interactive map that provides personalized recommendations for tourists and locals alike — helping users discover nearby producers, artisans, and products that would otherwise go unnoticed. The app is built to actively promote and strengthen local commerce in communities that rely on it.

---

### 🔭 Data Orbit NASA — AI-Powered Scientific Discovery
> *Web application · RAG Architecture · Vector Graph Database · LLM Integration*

A web platform that makes NASA's official scientific research truly accessible to everyone. Data Orbit NASA is built on a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture powered by a graph-based vector database, where NASA articles are pre-processed, embedded, and optimized for semantic search and fast retrieval. Users can explore, query, and understand complex scientific content through natural language — with every answer backed by direct, verifiable references to the original NASA sources. No hallucinations, no vague summaries: just accurate, source-traceable scientific knowledge made human.

---

### 🏛️ Verano Regional de la Ciencia — Scientific Summer Platform
> *Full-stack Web System · Multi-role · Institutional · Mexico*

A comprehensive web system that connects institutions, researchers, and students for the annual celebration of summer science and engineering projects across Mexico's central region. The platform manages the full lifecycle of the event: project submission and registration, institutional coordination, reviewer assignments, and public showcasing of scientific work. Designed to streamline what was once a logistically complex academic event into a unified, accessible digital experience that elevates the quality and reach of student scientific participation.
